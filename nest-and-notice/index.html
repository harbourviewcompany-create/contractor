The Notice List

We clean the obvious areas. Then we check the details people actually notice.

Most cleaning services say they are thorough. Nest & Notice defines what thorough means room by room: counter edges, mirror corners, fixture shine, floor edges, cushion resets, entryway impressions and the small details that make a home feel properly finished.

Kitchen Details

The kitchen is where clean has to feel visible. We focus on the edges, touchpoints and finishes that shape the whole room.

  • Appliance fronts
  • Sink finish

  • Floor corners

Bathroom & Fixture Details

Bathrooms need more than a wipe-down. We check the corners, chrome, mirror edges and surfaces that show whether the room was truly finished.

Questions

A few things people ask before booking.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No. Many clients provide access instructions, a lockbox, concierge details or smart-lock entry.

Do you bring supplies?

The site can support either option. Some clients prefer company-provided supplies, while others request specific in-home products.

What is included in a Home Reset?

A Home Reset usually includes kitchen, bathrooms, floors, surfaces and a final Notice pass.

Can I book recurring visits?

Yes. Recurring Nest Care can be weekly, biweekly or monthly depending on the home and schedule.

Do you handle guest turnovers?

Yes. Guest-Ready Turnover is designed for hosts who need the next arrival to feel intentional.
